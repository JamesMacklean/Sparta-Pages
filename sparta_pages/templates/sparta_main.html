About Project SPARTA

Project SPARTA aims to put in place the necessary online education, R&D mechanisms, and infrastructure not only to enable the industry of data science and analytics, but also to foster smart governance practices. The projects aims to graduate learners on various learning pathways in the field of data science and analytics through the developed online courses within a three-year project period.

Become a SPARTA Scholar

Interested applicants must register at the official SPARTA Application Portal. After registering and logging in at the Portal, applicants will be required to submit personal information and eligibility documents. Once eligible, scholars may apply for Learning Pathways, each with a unique track of data analytics-related courses.
