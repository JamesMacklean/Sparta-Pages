About Project SPARTA

Project SPARTA was launched in order to put in place the necessary online education, research and development mechanisms and infrastracture to not only enable the industry of data science and analytics but also to advance smart governance practices. It aims to train and graduate 30,000 learners in data science, analytics, and related fields through online learning.
